Unpopular opinion: most AI automation projects fail because of trust, not technology. 🎯

Every AI action in my system requires human approval before execution.
Emails go through HITL. LinkedIn posts go through HITL. Everything sensitive.

Yes, it's slower. But it's also:
✅ Auditable
✅ Recoverable
✅ Trustworthy

The goal isn't full autonomy on day one — it's building trust incrementally.
